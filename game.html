SpaceCat

What, is that a cat exploring the universe?

You're right, yes, the cat is on her way to the next planet!

Join the cosmic escapades of "Space Cat," a delightful 2D challenge game. Embark on an interstellar escapade as an inquisitive cat, meandering through the vastness of space between three distinct planets. Ready to execute thrilling catapult maneuvers!

Get ready to be captivated by the enchanting tale of Space Cat, where adventure, skill, and determination meld together to create an unforgettable gaming experience. Are you up for the challenge? The cosmos awaits your cat prowess!
